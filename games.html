Genshin Impact

Anime style game.

Genshin Impact is set in the fantasy world of Teyvat, which is home to seven different nations, each associated with a different element and ruled by a different god, called "The Archon" in the game's universe. The story follows the Traveler as he travels through countless worlds with his twin brother before becoming separated in Teyvat. Together with his companion Paimon, the Traveler goes in search of his lost brother, while simultaneously participating in the affairs of the peoples of Teyvat.
